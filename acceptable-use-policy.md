# AI Acceptable Use Policy
## K-12 District IT Helpdesk AI Agent — Staff Policy Template

**Version:** 1.0
**Applies To:** All district staff using the AI IT Helpdesk Agent
**Review Cycle:** Annual

---

## Purpose

This policy governs staff use of the district's AI-powered IT Helpdesk Agent ("the Agent"), built on Microsoft Copilot Studio. It establishes expectations for appropriate use, defines prohibited behaviors, and ensures the Agent is used in a manner consistent with district policy, FERPA, and applicable law.

---

## What the Agent Is

The AI Helpdesk Agent is an **automated IT support tool** that:

- Helps staff resolve common IT issues (password resets, device troubleshooting, M365 support)
- Answers IT-related questions using the district's approved knowledge base
- Creates and routes support tickets to human technicians when needed
- Is available 24/7 via Microsoft Teams and the district intranet

The Agent is **not** a general-purpose AI assistant. It is scoped exclusively to IT support topics.

---

## Acceptable Use

Staff may use the Agent to:

✅ Reset or recover their own district-issued account credentials
✅ Troubleshoot district-issued devices and software
✅ Get help with Microsoft 365 applications (Teams, Outlook, SharePoint, OneDrive)
✅ Submit IT support tickets
✅ Access approved IT knowledge base articles and guides
✅ Get onboarding assistance for new staff accounts and software access

---

## Prohibited Use

Staff may **not** use the Agent to:

❌ Attempt to access another staff member's account or data
❌ Submit requests on behalf of students or access student records through the Agent
❌ Attempt to bypass security controls, MFA requirements, or Conditional Access policies
❌ Input sensitive personal information (SSNs, financial data, health records) into Agent conversations
❌ Use the Agent for non-IT-related inquiries (HR, payroll, facilities)
❌ Attempt to manipulate the Agent into performing actions outside its defined scope
❌ Share Agent responses externally without IT department approval

---

## Data & Privacy

- All Agent interactions are authenticated — your identity is known and logged
- **Conversation content is not retained** after your session ends
- Metadata (interaction date, topic category, resolution status) is retained for 90 days for quality improvement
- The Agent operates entirely within the district's Microsoft 365 tenant — no data leaves the district's contracted cloud environment
- The Agent does not have access to student data under any circumstances

---

- **Phone:** [District IT Help Line]

The Agent will automatically escalate to a human technician for any interaction it identifies as a potential security incident.

---

## Compliance

Use of the Agent is subject to:

- District Technology Acceptable Use Policy
- Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- California Education Code §49073 et seq.
- Microsoft's Education Privacy Commitments and DPA

---

## Violations

Violations of this policy may result in:

- Suspension of Agent access
- Review by district IT and HR departments
- Disciplinary action per district employee handbook
- Referral to legal counsel where applicable
